Zhaohui Chen, Ph.D.

Professor, Deputy Director of Physical Oceanography Laboratory, Ocean University of China

Executive Dean of Graduate School, Ocean University of China

I am a physical oceanographer at Frontier Science Center for Deep Ocean Multispheres and Earth System (FDOMES) and Physical Oceanography Laboratory, Ocean University of China, Qingdao, China. More specifically, I focus on:

  • Ocean observing technologies and scientific applications in the Northwestern Pacific
  • Multi-scale oceanic processes and their roles in climate, ecosystem and fisheries
  • Dynamics of global western boundary currents and their roles in climate
